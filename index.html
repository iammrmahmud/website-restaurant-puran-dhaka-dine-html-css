Our Story and Guest Review

Our Story

Bringing the Taste of Puran Dhaka to America

Puran Dhaka Dine was created to bring the rich food culture of Old Dhaka to guests in America who miss the comfort of home and to new diners who want to experience authentic Bengali cuisine. Our kitchen is inspired by family recipes, timeless spice blends, and the warm hospitality that makes Bengali dining feel personal and memorable.

We proudly serve 100% halal food, prepared with quality ingredients and careful attention to tradition. From fragrant biryani to hearty rice dishes and celebratory family platters, every meal is designed to honor heritage while offering a clean, welcoming, and modern restaurant experience.

Our goal is simple: to create a place where culture, flavor, and community meet at one table. Whether you visit for breakfast with family, lunch with coworkers, or dinner for a special occasion, Puran Dhaka Dine is here to serve you with care, respect, and unforgettable taste.
